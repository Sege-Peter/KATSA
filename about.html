Who We Are

KATSA is a dynamic student-led association uniting young scholars and alumni from Kanyaluo Ward. We’re dedicated to academic growth, cultural pride, youth empowerment, and community transformation.

Our Vision

To build a vibrant and cohesive community of Kanyaluo students, united by commitment to academic success, cultural heritage, and social transformation.

Our Mission

To foster academic excellence, promote cultural identity, and empower students through mentorship, leadership development, and service to the community.

Our Motto

Empowering Kanyaluo Minds — a call to action for young leaders to rise with knowledge, unity, and pride.
